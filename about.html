About Us

The Drinkers Nepal, established in 2021, is Nepal’s premier wine importer, bar, retailer, and online shop. Our mission is to revolutionize the wine culture in Nepal by making world-class wines accessible, enjoyable, and educational for all.

We believe wine is more than a drink—it's a lifestyle, a culture, and a journey of discovery. That’s why we source only the finest wines directly from renowned vineyards, ensuring authenticity and value for our customers. Our regular wine tastings and training events, led by certified wine advisors, are designed to enrich your knowledge and elevate your experience.

At The Drinkers Nepal Bar, we pride ourselves on hospitality and ambiance. Enjoy a curated international wine list, craft beers, an extensive selection of whiskies, and signature cocktails—all paired with creative cuisine made from fresh, local produce. Our menu is crafted for sharing, making every visit memorable.

  • Handpicked wine lists from the world’s best producers
  • Seasonal selections and exclusive offers
  • Direct import pricing—no middlemen, best value
  • Expert-led wine tastings and educational events
  • Daily promotions and special sales
  • Seamless online and in-store shopping
  • Passionate staff, exceptional service, and a welcoming atmosphere

Whether you are a wine enthusiast or a curious beginner, The Drinkers Nepal is your trusted partner for discovering, learning, and enjoying the world of wine and fine beverages in Nepal.
